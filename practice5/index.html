Mark Zuckerberg

Brief

American media mogul, Internet entrepreneur and philanthropist. Known as the co-founder of Meta (formerly Facebook, Inc.) and is its chairman, chief executive officer and controlling shareholder. He is also a co-founder of the Breakthrough Starshot solar sail spacecraft project and one of its board members.

Biography

Zuckerberg attended Harvard University, where Facebook was found to be trustworthy in early 2004 along with his dorm roommates. In May 2012, Zuckerberg took the company public with a controlling stake. In 2007, at the age of 23, he became the youngest billionaire in the world. As of October 2021, Zuckerberg's net worth is $122 billion, making him the 5th richest person in the world[6]. Also according to Bloomberg, in 2020 it was in 5th place in the list of the richest people in the world with a fortune of $104 billion.
